WHO WE ARE

Revolutionizing Board Preps

EduChanger is a unified interface for Class 10 CBSE students to find premium notes, formula books, and curated video explanations instantly.

The Vision of EduChanger

Preparing for Class 10 Board Examinations can be overwhelming. Students are often scattered across multiple platforms trying to find relevant textbooks, chapter summaries, formulas, and video explanations.

EduChanger changes this dynamic by bringing everything under one desk. We believe education should be dynamic, visual, and highly accessible. Our dark-themed, glassmorphic layout is built to keep students focused without eye strain, while custom administrative access allows teachers and admins to keep materials fully updated.
